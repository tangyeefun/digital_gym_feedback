Strength Training

Use these exercises to build strength and improve your balance for all the balance activities (one point, two points, three points and four points).

Exercises

  1. Skip a rope (10 counts) Hold the rope handles, swing the rope over your head and jump as it passes your feet. Count 10 skips.
  2. Alternate superman pose (5 counts each side) Lie on your tummy. Lift your right arm and left leg together, hold briefly, then switch to left arm and right leg. Count 5 lifts each side.
  3. Alternate single leg balance (10 counts each side) Stand tall and lift one leg off the floor. Try to balance while counting slowly to 10, then switch legs.
  4. Planking on floor (10 counts) Place your forearms on the floor, legs straight, body in a straight line. Hold the plank while counting to 10.
  5. Plank with apparatus (10 counts) Place your hands or forearms on a stable apparatus (e.g. step or low box) and hold a plank position while counting to 10.
After you complete these exercises, go back to the balance pages and try the balances again. You can use the Home button to choose any balance task (one point, two points, three points or four points).
